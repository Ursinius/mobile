JollyOn (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a platform to discover South African pubs, bars, clubs, and events, and for venue owners to list venues and events. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use the JollyOn mobile application and website (collectively, the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Data type Examples Collected? Purpose
Account / identity Email address, password (stored hashed), venue-owner flag Yes (required to register) Authentication, account management, owner features
User content / activity RSVPs, followed venues, events you create (owners) Yes (when you use features) Provide core app functionality
Precise / approximate location Latitude and longitude when you enable “Near Me” Yes (optional — only with your permission) Show nearby venues and events within your chosen radius
Device / technical IP address, request timestamps, app/API logs Yes (automatically) Security, fraud prevention, reliability, diagnostics

We do not use location for advertising, we do not sell location data, and you can disable location by turning off Near Me or revoking permission in your device settings.

3. How we use your information

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you
  • Display events, venues, RSVPs, and owner dashboard features you request
  • Perform location-based search when you opt in to Near Me
  • Send transactional emails (e.g. account deletion confirmation) when email is configured
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the Service
  • Comply with applicable law

4. Legal basis (South Africa — POPIA)

Where the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) applies, we process personal information based on: your consent (e.g. optional location), performance of a contract (providing the Service you signed up for), and our legitimate interests (security and service improvement), balanced against your rights.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g. cloud servers) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations
  • Payment processor: PayFast, when venue owners complete a subscription payment on PayFast’s platform (subject to PayFast’s privacy policy)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice where required by law

6. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymise associated personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, tax, audit, or security purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information, including HTTPS for data in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls on our servers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on applicable law (including POPIA), you may have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you

Account deletion: In the app, go to Profile → Delete account, or email nicovd01@gmail.com. Deletion may require email confirmation. Some data may remain in backups for a limited time.

9. Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. JollyOn lists nightlife venues and events intended for adults. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.
