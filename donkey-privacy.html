Donkey — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026 · App package: com.donkey.game

Donkey (“the App”) is a local multiplayer party card game. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the App.

Summary

  • No accounts or sign-in.
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • Selfies are used only during a live game session and are not saved to permanent storage by the App for later use.
  • Game traffic stays on your local Wi‑Fi network between players’ devices.

Information we process

In-game photos (selfies)

If you grant camera or photo-library permission, the App may capture or select a photo to use as your player image during a game. Photos are kept in device memory for the session, may be shared over the local network only with other players in the same room, and are cleared when the game session ends or you leave.

Display name

The name you type is shown to other players in the same room over the local network and is not uploaded to our servers (the App does not use a cloud backend for play).

Network data

On a local Wi‑Fi network, devices exchange game state (cards, scores, room codes, photos for the current session) so multiplayer can work. This communication is peer-to-host on your LAN.

Permissions

  • Camera / photos — optional selfie for gameplay.
  • Network / Wi‑Fi — find rooms and play with friends nearby.

Children

The App is a casual party game suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children for marketing or profiles.

Changes

We may update this policy when the App changes. The “Last updated” date above will change accordingly.

Contact

For privacy questions about this App, contact the developer via the email address listed on the Google Play store listing for Donkey (com.donkey.game).
