Privacy Policy

App: FastTrack (package com.jacobie.fasttrack)
Last updated: July 13, 2026

Introduction

FastTrack (“the App”) is a personal intermittent fasting tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used. By using FastTrack, you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • Your fasting sessions, body metrics, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • The App does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The App does not sell your personal data.
  • The current version has no built-in cloud sync.

Data stored on your device

Depending on how you use the App, FastTrack may store:

  • Fast session history — start and end times, fast type, duration
  • Optional body metrics — height and weight entries you choose to log
  • App preferences — for example, metric vs imperial units
  • Related app state needed for features such as streaks and badges

This information is saved in a local database on your phone or tablet. Uninstalling the App or clearing the App’s storage typically deletes this local data.

Data we do not collect (current version)

  • No email, password, or user account system
  • No advertising ID collection by the App itself
  • No built-in third-party analytics or crash-reporting cloud service in the current App
  • No sharing of health or fasting data with third-party cloud APIs by the App

If you download the App from Google Play or use device system services, those providers process information under their own privacy policies (for example, install statistics or crash reports handled by the platform).

How we use information

On-device data is used only to provide App features you request, such as:

  • Running and displaying your active fast and history
  • Showing educational phase and refeed guidance
  • Calculating streaks, badges, and optional BMI from values you enter
  • Remembering your unit and profile preferences

Permissions

FastTrack is designed to work with minimal permissions. If a future version requests additional permissions (for example, notifications), they will be used only for features you enable and will be disclosed at the time of request.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell personal data. The current version does not transmit your fasting log or body metrics to our servers. We do not share that on-device data with third parties for advertising.

Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the App (where available), clear the App’s storage in system settings, or uninstall the App. Because data is local, there is no separate cloud account to delete with us.

Children’s privacy

FastTrack is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region for this type of app). Do not use the App if you are under the applicable age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Medical disclaimer

FastTrack is for educational and personal tracking purposes only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Fasting can be unsafe for some people. Always consult your doctor or a qualified healthcare professional before starting, changing, or ending a fast — especially if you are pregnant, take medication, have a medical condition, or have a history of eating disorders.

Security

We rely on your device’s storage and operating system security for local data. You can further protect your device with a screen lock and OS security updates. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

International users

The App stores data on the device you use. Processing occurs on that device. If you use the App outside your home country, local laws may also apply to your use of the App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App or our practices change. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions about FastTrack, contact the developer using the email address listed on the FastTrack Google Play store listing, or the support contact published with the App.
