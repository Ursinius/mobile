PictureIt Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 July 2026 · App ID: com.jacobie.pictureit

PictureIt (“we”, “the app”) is a local, offline-friendly mobile app that lets you photograph clouds, draw what you see, and play a pass-and-play guessing game with friends or family on the same device.

Safety reminder: Never stare into the sun when looking at the sky or taking photos.

1. Information we collect

PictureIt is designed to keep your data on your device.

  • Photos you take: Cloud photos captured with the camera stay on your device (app cache and app storage). They are used only so you can draw on them and play the friend challenge.
  • Drawings: Composite images you create are saved only in the app’s local storage on your device.
  • Game names & guesses: Friend names, guesses, and correct/incorrect marks for a challenge session are kept in memory during play and are not uploaded.
  • Preferences: Simple on-device preferences (for example, whether you completed the in-app tour) may be stored locally.

We do not require an account. We do not operate a backend that receives your photos or drawings.

2. Permissions

  • Camera: Required to photograph clouds for drawing and friend challenges.

You can revoke camera access in your device settings. Without camera access, photo features will not work.

3. How we use information

On-device media and game state are used solely to provide PictureIt features:

  • Display and draw on cloud photos
  • Pass-and-play friend challenges (guess and mark correct/incorrect)
  • Remember that the tour was completed

4. Sharing

The app does not upload your photos or drawings to our servers. If a future version reintroduces system share sheets, any sharing would be initiated by you through the device’s OS share UI to an app you choose.

5. Children

PictureIt is a family-friendly cloud-drawing game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online. Parents and guardians should supervise camera use and outdoor safety (including sun safety).

6. Data retention & deletion

Photos and drawings remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the app’s storage. Uninstalling PictureIt removes local app data according to your operating system.

7. Security

Because processing is local, your cloud photos and drawings are protected by your device’s normal security (lock screen, OS sandbox). Keep your device updated and protected.

8. Third parties

PictureIt does not include third-party advertising SDKs in the base app. Platform services (Google Play, Apple App Store) may collect install and crash diagnostics according to their own policies when you use their stores.

9. Changes

We may update this policy when the app’s data practices change. The effective date at the top will be revised.
